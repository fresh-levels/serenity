  • Additional Description

    BO/BB: This stands for "Bed Only" or "Bed and Breakfast." With this meal plan, guests receive accommodations (the bed) and breakfast as part of their stay. Any other meals, such as lunch and dinner, are not included and are typically paid for separately.

    HB/FB: This stands for "Half Board" and "Full Board." Half Board (HB) includes accommodations, breakfast, and one other meal, usually dinner, as part of the room rate. Guests are responsible for their own lunch. Full Board (FB) includes accommodations, breakfast, lunch, and dinner as part of the room rate. Guests typically have all their meals provided.
